DEFINITION OF A PHENOTYPE SPAN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
A phenotype span is a contiguous or minimally reformulated text fragment that:
• describes an abnormality, clinical sign, symptom, structural variation, or functional deviation
• may include relevant modifiers (severity, frequency, negation, laterality, age, context)
• may be implicit if clearly inferable from the sentence
• must NOT include normal findings unless they are explicitly informative
